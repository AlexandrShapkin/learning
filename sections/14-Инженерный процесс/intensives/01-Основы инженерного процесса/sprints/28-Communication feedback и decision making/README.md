# Спринт 28. Communication, feedback и decision making

Ориентировочный объём: 18–27 часов.

## Центральный вопрос

Как передавать context, uncertainty, disagreement and decisions with minimal loss and coordination cost?

## Результат спринта

После завершения необходимо уметь:

- choose synchronous versus asynchronous communication;
- structure decision requests and status by audience;
- communicate facts, hypotheses, risks and asks separately;
- give specific behavior/impact feedback;
- receive and resolve technical feedback without personalization;
- facilitate disagreement and consent/commitment;
- record decisions and action ownership;
- run useful meetings with explicit purpose and output;
- avoid information broadcast without decision path;
- manage escalation and bad-news communication early.

## Основной маршрут

1. Rewrite vague engineering messages into decision-ready communication.
2. Conduct asynchronous design disagreement.
3. Run one timeboxed decision meeting.
4. Deliver difficult feedback and early risk escalation in synthetic scenarios.

## Лабораторная работа

Создать `work/communication-decisions/`.

### Requirements

- communication names audience, purpose and required action;
- facts, interpretations and unknowns are distinct;
- decision proposal includes options, recommendation and deadline;
- meeting has agenda, decision owner and recorded output;
- feedback describes observed behavior, impact and requested change;
- dissent and unresolved risk are preserved;
- escalation occurs on named threshold, not personal frustration;
- important decision does not live only in chat/meeting memory;
- notification volume is reduced through better routing.

### Failure scenarios

1. Status message contains activity but no impact/risk/ask.
2. Meeting has no decision owner or output.
3. Technical disagreement becomes authority contest.
4. Bad news is delayed to avoid discomfort.
5. Feedback attacks personality rather than behavior.
6. Decision is repeatedly reopened because rationale is absent.
7. Everyone is copied on every message.

## Результат для базы знаний

- **Engineering communication by audience and purpose**.
- **Asynchronous and synchronous decision making**.
- **Technical disagreement and recorded decisions**.
- **Engineering feedback and escalation**.

## Когда переходить дальше

Можно переходить, когда a contested decision reaches a clear outcome with preserved evidence, dissent, ownership and next actions.
