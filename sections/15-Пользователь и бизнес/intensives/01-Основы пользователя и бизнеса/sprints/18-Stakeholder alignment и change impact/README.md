# Спринт 18. Stakeholder alignment и change impact

Ориентировочный объём: 18–27 часов.

## Центральный вопрос

Как согласовать ожидания и подготовить роли, процессы и incentives to a change without confusing agreement with absence of conflict?

## Результат спринта

После завершения необходимо уметь:

- identify affected roles and change magnitude;
- analyze gains, losses, incentives and resistance;
- distinguish information, consultation, decision and approval;
- map changes to tasks, skills, access, policy and metrics;
- create stakeholder engagement and communication plan;
- preserve dissent and unresolved risk;
- define training, migration and support needs;
- manage sponsor and local-champion assumptions;
- measure readiness rather than meeting attendance;
- avoid forced consensus and checkbox communication.

## Основной маршрут

1. Build impact map for the to-be process.
2. Analyze incentives and likely resistance.
3. Define engagement/decision path.
4. Create readiness and support evidence.

## Лабораторная работа

Создать `work/change-impact/`.

### Requirements

- each affected role has changed tasks, permissions and success measures;
- losses and workload shifts are explicit;
- decision rights and consultation boundaries are clear;
- dissent is recorded with disposition;
- training/support/access/data migration needs are linked to impact;
- sponsor/champion roles have responsibilities and limits;
- readiness measures behavior/capability, not message delivery;
- rollback or coexistence is considered for adoption failure.

### Failure scenarios

1. Stakeholders are marked aligned after one meeting.
2. New process increases worker effort while manager benefit dominates.
3. Local incentives encourage bypass.
4. Training is generic feature presentation.
5. Dissent disappears from final decision record.
6. Communication plan has no feedback or readiness measure.

## Результат для базы знаний

- **Stakeholder alignment and decision participation**.
- **Organizational change-impact analysis**.
- **Incentives, resistance and readiness**.
- **Change communication, training and support needs**.

## Когда переходить дальше

Можно переходить, когда affected roles, incentives, changed work and readiness gaps are explicit and no conflict is hidden under nominal agreement.
