# Спринт 01. Пользователи, клиенты и заинтересованные стороны

Ориентировочный объём: 16–23 часа.

## Центральный вопрос

Кто использует, покупает, оплачивает, принимает решение и несёт последствия продукта?

## Результат спринта

После завершения необходимо уметь:

- различать user, customer, buyer, sponsor, administrator and process owner;
- выявлять direct, indirect and negatively affected stakeholders;
- анализировать goals, incentives, authority and constraints;
- строить stakeholder map and decision-right map;
- находить конфликтующие интересы;
- определять owner of outcome and owner of data/process;
- избегать предположения, что requester represents every user.

## Основной маршрут

1. Inventory all roles around the field-service workflow.
2. Separate economic buyer, daily users and affected customers.
3. Map power, interest, incentives and decision rights.
4. Identify conflicts and missing voices.

## Лабораторная работа

Создать `work/stakeholders/`.

### Requirements

- role map covers use, payment, approval, support, data and risk;
- each role has goals, pains, authority and success criteria;
- affected non-users are included;
- conflicts and trade-offs are explicit;
- one role is not treated as a persona without evidence;
- outcome ownership and escalation are defined;
- stakeholder assumptions have validation methods.

### Failure scenarios

1. Buyer is assumed to be the main user.
2. Administrator/support roles are omitted.
3. Customer goals conflict with worker incentives silently.
4. Requester is treated as sole source of truth.
5. No one owns the business outcome.
6. Affected external parties are absent.

## Результат для базы знаний

- **Users, customers, buyers and stakeholders**.
- **Stakeholder goals, incentives and influence**.
- **Outcome, process and data ownership**.
- **Stakeholder conflicts and decision rights**.

## Когда переходить дальше

Можно переходить, когда product decisions can be traced to distinct roles and no critical affected group is represented only by assumption.
