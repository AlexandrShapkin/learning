# Спринт 11. Strategy, roadmap и prioritization

Ориентировочный объём: 19–28 часов.

## Центральный вопрос

Какие strategic choices and product bets should be made now, and what should deliberately not be pursued?

## Результат спринта

После завершения необходимо уметь:

- distinguish strategy from goals, plans and feature lists;
- identify diagnosis, guiding policy and coherent actions;
- define strategic choices and exclusions;
- create outcome-oriented roadmap;
- prioritize opportunities and bets by value, evidence, risk and timing;
- manage dependencies and capacity constraints;
- use scoring methods critically;
- express roadmap confidence and decision points;
- update strategy when assumptions fail;
- avoid promise-driven roadmap theater.

## Основной маршрут

1. Diagnose the product/business challenge.
2. Choose target segment and strategic focus.
3. Create 3–5 outcome bets with assumptions.
4. Sequence them into a decision roadmap.
5. Inject new evidence and revise choices.

## Лабораторная работа

Создать `work/product-strategy/`.

### Requirements

- strategy includes explicit diagnosis and trade-offs;
- roadmap items are outcomes/bets, not feature commitments;
- each bet has evidence, assumption, risk and decision threshold;
- capacity and opportunity cost are explicit;
- excluded segments/features are named;
- dependencies and sequencing support learning;
- confidence decreases with horizon;
- new evidence can stop or reorder work.

### Failure scenarios

1. Strategy is a list of goals and features.
2. Roadmap promises dates without uncertainty.
3. Every stakeholder request remains on roadmap.
4. Scoring formula hides political weights.
5. No-build or stop option is absent.
6. Long-horizon items are presented as commitments.

## Результат для базы знаний

- **Product strategy as choices and coherent actions**.
- **Outcome-oriented product roadmaps**.
- **Product bets, assumptions and decision thresholds**.
- **Opportunity cost and strategic exclusions**.

## Когда переходить дальше

Можно переходить, когда roadmap choices can be explained by one coherent strategy and changing evidence can alter or stop a bet.
