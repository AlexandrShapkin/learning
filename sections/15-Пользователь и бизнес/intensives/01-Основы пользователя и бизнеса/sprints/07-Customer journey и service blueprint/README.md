# Спринт 07. Customer journey и service blueprint

Ориентировочный объём: 19–28 часов.

## Центральный вопрос

Как пользовательский опыт связан с backstage processes, systems, policies and support?

## Результат спринта

После завершения необходимо уметь:

- map journey stages, goals, actions, touchpoints and emotions;
- identify moments of truth, abandonment and recovery;
- distinguish journey map from process flow;
- build service blueprint with frontstage/backstage/support layers;
- map evidence, actors, systems and handoffs;
- represent waiting, exceptions and failure recovery;
- quantify pain without inventing certainty;
- connect journey issues to operational causes;
- prioritize experience improvements by impact and feasibility.

## Основной маршрут

1. Map request-to-completion journey for customer and worker.
2. Add channels, wait states, failures and support contacts.
3. Build corresponding service blueprint.
4. Identify root operational causes behind three journey pains.

## Лабораторная работа

Создать `work/journey-blueprint/`.

### Requirements

- journey has start/end and segment/context;
- touchpoints include human, digital and offline channels;
- evidence quality is marked;
- abandonment, retries and recovery paths are shown;
- blueprint maps frontstage, backstage, systems and policies;
- ownership and handoffs are explicit;
- at least one visible pain is traced to an invisible process/control;
- improvement opportunities include user and operational effects.

### Failure scenarios

1. Journey is an idealized linear happy path.
2. Emotions are invented without evidence.
3. Support and offline work are omitted.
4. Frontend redesign is proposed for a backstage policy problem.
5. Wait time and handoffs are hidden.
6. Different segments are merged into one average journey.

## Результат для базы знаний

- **Customer journey maps**.
- **Moments of truth, abandonment and recovery**.
- **Service blueprints and frontstage/backstage layers**.
- **Journey-to-process causal linkage**.

## Когда переходить дальше

Можно переходить, когда key user pains can be traced through touchpoints to specific backstage processes, systems and owners.
