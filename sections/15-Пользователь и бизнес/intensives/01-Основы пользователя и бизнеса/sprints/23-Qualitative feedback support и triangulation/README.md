# Спринт 23. Qualitative feedback, support и triangulation

Ориентировочный объём: 18–27 часов.

## Центральный вопрос

Как объединять analytics, support signals and research evidence without letting one loud channel define product truth?

## Результат спринта

После завершения необходимо уметь:

- create taxonomy for support/contact reasons;
- distinguish volume, severity, frequency and affected value;
- detect selection bias in feedback channels;
- link tickets, interviews and events at aggregate level;
- triangulate converging and conflicting evidence;
- identify silent failure and non-reporting users;
- separate request, root need and proposed solution;
- measure support burden and deflection carefully;
- close feedback loops without promising every request;
- protect personal/sensitive support content.

## Основной маршрут

1. Classify synthetic support cases and feedback.
2. Link themes to journey/funnel/process points.
3. Compare with analytics and research.
4. Prioritize one issue and reject one loud but low-impact request.

## Лабораторная работа

Создать `work/feedback-triangulation/`.

### Requirements

- taxonomy has mutually understandable categories and update rules;
- volume is weighted by severity, affected segment and business/user impact;
- channel/sample bias is explicit;
- sensitive details are removed before analysis;
- requests are translated into underlying jobs/problems;
- analytics and qualitative evidence are compared, not forced to agree;
- silent/non-contacting users are considered;
- support efficiency does not incentivize hiding unresolved issues;
- decision communicates evidence and uncertainty.

### Failure scenarios

1. Loud customer request overrides broader evidence.
2. Ticket count ignores severity and account size.
3. Category changes make trend incomparable.
4. Self-service deflection rises while task failure increases.
5. Support transcripts expose personal data.
6. Contradictory analytics and interviews are averaged away.

## Результат для базы знаний

- **Support and feedback taxonomies**.
- **Feedback-channel selection bias**.
- **Triangulation of qualitative and quantitative evidence**.
- **Support burden, severity and silent failure**.

## Когда переходить дальше

Можно переходить, когда a product decision is supported by multiple evidence types and channel bias, contradictions and silent users remain visible.
