# Спринт 13. Проверка продуктовой стратегии

Ориентировочный объём: 15–22 часа.

Новая теория не вводится. Первая попытка выполняется без перечитывания спринтов 09–12.

## Исходная задача

Создать `work/product-strategy-checkpoint/` — evidence-based product bet with value proposition, outcome model, strategy, roadmap and bounded validation.

## Required artifacts

- target segment, job and alternatives;
- value proposition and positioning;
- product vision and strategic diagnosis;
- outcome/metric tree with guardrails;
- prioritized bets and exclusions;
- roadmap with confidence and decision points;
- prototype/MVP/pilot plan and result;
- continue, adapt or stop decision.

## Mandatory failure cases

1. Value proposition is a feature list.
2. Target segment is everyone.
3. Buyer and user value conflict silently.
4. Goal is an output rather than outcome.
5. Metric target creates harmful behavior.
6. Metric tree hides causal assumptions.
7. Strategy is only goals/features.
8. Roadmap is a promise list.
9. Opportunity cost and exclusions are absent.
10. Prototype usability is interpreted as viability.
11. Pilot sample contains only enthusiasts.
12. Success criteria change after results.

## Defense

1. For whom and against which alternative is the product valuable?
2. Which evidence supports differentiation?
3. What is the strategic diagnosis and guiding policy?
4. Which outcomes matter and why?
5. Which guardrails prevent local optimization?
6. Which causal assumptions connect metrics?
7. What is deliberately excluded?
8. Why are bets sequenced this way?
9. Which uncertainty is tested first?
10. Why was the validation method appropriate?
11. What does the result not prove?
12. Why continue, adapt or stop?

## Критерии прохождения

- value and positioning are segment-specific and evidence-linked;
- strategy contains choices and coherent actions;
- goals and metrics balance user/business outcomes and guardrails;
- roadmap expresses bets and uncertainty;
- validation resolves a critical assumption ethically;
- all twelve failure cases are identified or corrected;
- at least 10 of 12 defense answers cite evidence;
- outcome ends with an explicit decision rather than automatic continuation.

## Результат для базы знаний

Checkpoint-specific note не требуется. Update value/strategy/metrics/validation notes where the exercise changed assumptions.
