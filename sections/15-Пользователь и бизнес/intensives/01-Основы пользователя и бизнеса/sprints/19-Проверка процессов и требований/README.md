# Спринт 19. Проверка процессов и требований

Ориентировочный объём: 15–22 часа.

Новая теория не вводится. Первая попытка выполняется без перечитывания спринтов 14–18.

## Исходная задача

Создать `work/process-requirements-checkpoint/` — complete as-is/to-be business analysis with rules, authoritative data, requirements and organizational change impact.

## Required artifacts

- as-is and to-be process models;
- wait, rework, handoff and exception evidence;
- decision tables and rule ownership;
- domain glossary, events and state transitions;
- data/source-of-truth map;
- business, user and system requirements;
- solution-option and impact analysis;
- stakeholder alignment/readiness plan.

## Mandatory failure cases

1. Process map shows only screens.
2. Ideal policy replaces actual as-is behavior.
3. Exceptions and manual work are omitted.
4. Automation preserves unnecessary approval.
5. Conflicting rules have no precedence.
6. Override is permanent or unaudited.
7. Critical business term has different meanings.
8. Reporting copy becomes source of truth silently.
9. Requirement embeds unnecessary implementation.
10. Business outcome is not traceable to system acceptance.
11. Stakeholder alignment hides incentives or dissent.
12. Training/readiness is measured only by attendance.

## Defense

1. What triggers and completes the process?
2. Where are wait, rework and handoffs?
3. Which control can be removed, retained or redesigned?
4. Who owns each rule and exception?
5. Which event/state definitions are authoritative?
6. How are conflicting copies reconciled?
7. How do business requirements map to user/system behavior?
8. Which requirements cover failure, data and operations?
9. What solution options were rejected and why?
10. Which roles gain or lose work/authority?
11. How is readiness measured?
12. What transition risks remain?

## Критерии прохождения

- as-is reflects observed work and to-be shows explicit change;
- rules, decisions and data authorities are explainable;
- requirements trace from outcome to acceptance;
- stakeholder impacts and incentives are visible;
- all twelve failure cases are identified or corrected;
- at least 10 of 12 defense answers cite models or evidence;
- no duplicate source of truth is created by the artifact set.

## Результат для базы знаний

Checkpoint-specific note не требуется. Update process/rules/data/requirements/change notes where the exercise changed assumptions.
