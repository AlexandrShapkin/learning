# Спринт 28. Business risks, compliance и continuity

Ориентировочный объём: 20–29 часов.

## Центральный вопрос

Какие operational, financial, legal, privacy and continuity risks can invalidate the product/business case or make rollout unacceptable?

## Результат спринта

После завершения необходимо уметь:

- identify risk scenarios, owners and affected outcomes;
- distinguish likelihood, impact, exposure and uncertainty;
- perform business impact analysis;
- define critical processes, maximum tolerable disruption and recovery needs;
- identify privacy, contractual and regulatory constraints;
- map third-party/vendor concentration and exit risk;
- evaluate reputational and adoption risk;
- choose avoid, reduce, transfer or accept responses;
- define controls, evidence and residual risk;
- avoid treating compliance checklist as business assurance.

## Основной маршрут

1. Create product/business risk register.
2. Perform business impact analysis for critical workflow.
3. Model vendor, data, outage and regulatory scenarios.
4. Define mitigation, continuity and acceptance decisions.

## Лабораторная работа

Создать `work/business-risk-continuity/`.

### Requirements

- risks are written as cause-event-consequence scenarios;
- owners and affected users/processes are explicit;
- critical process has disruption, data-loss and manual-workaround limits;
- privacy/legal assumptions are identified for specialist review;
- vendor concentration, portability and exit are modeled;
- control effectiveness and evidence are specified;
- residual risk and acceptance authority are explicit;
- continuity capacity and backlog recovery are included;
- one low-probability/high-impact scenario is stress-tested.

### Failure scenarios

1. Risk register contains generic labels only.
2. Compliance status is treated as proof of safety/value.
3. Manual workaround has no capacity estimate.
4. Critical vendor cannot be replaced/exported.
5. Privacy requirement appears after instrumentation/rollout.
6. Residual risk has no owner/acceptance.

## Результат для базы знаний

- **Business risk scenarios and ownership**.
- **Business impact analysis and continuity needs**.
- **Operational, regulatory, privacy and vendor risks**.
- **Risk treatment, controls and residual acceptance**.

## Когда переходить дальше

Можно переходить, когда critical business interruption and constraint scenarios have explicit impact, controls, recovery assumptions and acceptance authority.
