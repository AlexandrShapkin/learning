# Спринт 30. Customer success, support и product operations

Ориентировочный объём: 19–28 часов.

## Центральный вопрос

Как обеспечить sustained customer outcome, supportability and product learning after rollout?

## Результат спринта

После завершения необходимо уметь:

- distinguish customer support, success and product operations;
- define health signals and success milestones by segment;
- design proactive and reactive support paths;
- build knowledge and escalation flows;
- connect product, support, sales and operations feedback;
- measure time-to-value, adoption depth and outcome realization;
- identify at-risk accounts/users without intrusive surveillance;
- manage renewals, expansion and churn signals critically;
- avoid support deflection as sole success measure;
- define ownership for recurring operational product work.

## Основной маршрут

1. Define customer success plan for three segments.
2. Create support taxonomy, SLA/escalation and knowledge paths.
3. Build health model from product/outcome signals.
4. Simulate one at-risk account and feedback loop.

## Лабораторная работа

Создать `work/customer-success-ops/`.

### Requirements

- success outcome and milestone differ by segment/job;
- time-to-value and adoption depth are measurable;
- support channels and escalation match severity/context;
- health score components have causal rationale and limits;
- product telemetry and human feedback are combined;
- privacy and false-positive risk are explicit;
- repeated support problems route to product/process ownership;
- knowledge articles are validated against actual tasks;
- renewal/expansion does not override user harm or unresolved risk.

### Failure scenarios

1. Customer success is reduced to renewal reminders.
2. Health score rewards logins rather than achieved outcome.
3. Support deflection hides unresolved failure.
4. High-value customer bypasses safety/process controls permanently.
5. Product team receives only anecdotes or only dashboards.
6. Repeated ticket has no structural owner.

## Результат для базы знаний

- **Customer success outcomes and health signals**.
- **Support channels, severity and escalation**.
- **Product operations and feedback routing**.
- **Time-to-value, adoption depth and at-risk detection**.

## Когда переходить дальше

Можно переходить, когда sustained customer outcome, support evidence and product feedback form one accountable operating loop.
