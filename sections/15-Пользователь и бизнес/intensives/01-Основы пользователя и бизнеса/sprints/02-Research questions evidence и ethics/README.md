# Спринт 02. Research questions, evidence и ethics

Ориентировочный объём: 17–25 часов.

## Центральный вопрос

Какой evidence действительно нужен для решения, и как получить его этично и без лишнего сбора данных?

## Результат спринта

После завершения необходимо уметь:

- формулировать research questions before choosing a method;
- различать exploratory, evaluative and measurement research;
- определять evidence needed for a product decision;
- выбирать qualitative and quantitative methods;
- планировать sampling and recruitment assumptions;
- фиксировать consent, privacy and retention;
- избегать leading questions and confirmation bias;
- определять stop criteria and decision consequences;
- оценивать evidence strength and uncertainty.

## Основной маршрут

1. Convert three assumptions into research questions.
2. Match each question to method and sample.
3. Create consent/privacy and data-handling plan.
4. Define decision thresholds before collection.

## Лабораторная работа

Создать `work/research-plan/`.

### Requirements

- each question maps to a decision or hypothesis;
- method is justified by the type of evidence needed;
- sample inclusion/exclusion logic is explicit;
- recruitment does not rely only on convenient enthusiasts;
- consent covers purpose, recording, use and retention;
- sensitive data is minimized;
- analysis plan distinguishes facts, interpretations and unknowns;
- stopping and pivot criteria are written before results.

### Failure scenarios

1. Method is chosen because it is familiar, not appropriate.
2. Research question already assumes the solution.
3. Only internal stakeholders are interviewed about user behavior.
4. Consent or retention is absent.
5. Sample excludes non-adopters unintentionally.
6. Decision criteria are invented after seeing results.

## Результат для базы знаний

- **User-research questions and decision linkage**.
- **Qualitative and quantitative evidence selection**.
- **Research sampling and recruitment assumptions**.
- **Research ethics, consent and data minimization**.
- **Evidence strength and uncertainty**.

## Когда переходить дальше

Можно переходить, когда every planned session or dataset has a clear question, decision use, ethical boundary and predeclared interpretation rule.
