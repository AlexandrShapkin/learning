# Спринт 08. Проверка исследования пользователей

Ориентировочный объём: 15–22 часа.

Новая теория не вводится. Первая попытка выполняется без перечитывания спринтов 01–07.

## Исходная задача

Создать `work/user-research-checkpoint/` — evidence package for one field-service problem, covering stakeholders, mixed methods, segmentation, personas, journeys and service blueprint.

## Required artifacts

- stakeholder and decision-right map;
- research questions, methods, sample and ethics plan;
- synthetic interview/observation evidence;
- survey with sample limitations;
- actionable segments and jobs;
- evidence-based personas and scenarios;
- customer journey and service blueprint;
- prioritized problem hypotheses and unknowns.

## Mandatory failure cases

1. Buyer is treated as daily user.
2. Research question assumes the solution.
3. Interview asks future intent and sells the idea.
4. Observation ignores workarounds.
5. Survey uses convenience sample as population truth.
6. Small subgroup result is overprecise.
7. Segment is decorative demographics.
8. Persona has invented biography/quote.
9. Non-adopter is omitted.
10. Journey contains only happy path.
11. User pain is disconnected from backstage cause.
12. Consent, privacy or retention is absent.

## Defense

1. Who uses, buys, decides and owns the outcome?
2. Which research questions drive decisions?
3. Why were the selected methods appropriate?
4. What behavior was observed rather than claimed?
5. What are sample and evidence limitations?
6. Why are the segments actionable?
7. Which jobs and alternatives matter?
8. How are personas grounded in evidence?
9. Where does the journey fail or recover?
10. Which backstage process causes the pain?
11. Which belief changed after research?
12. What remains unknown and how will it be tested?

## Критерии прохождения

- role and stakeholder distinctions are explicit;
- research methods match questions and ethical constraints;
- evidence separates observation, interpretation and uncertainty;
- segments/personas/scenarios change decisions;
- journey and blueprint include failures, waits and support;
- all twelve failure cases are identified or corrected;
- at least 10 of 12 defense answers cite evidence artifacts;
- no personal or real company data is stored.

## Результат для базы знаний

Checkpoint-specific note не требуется. Update research/segmentation/journey notes where the exercise changed assumptions.
