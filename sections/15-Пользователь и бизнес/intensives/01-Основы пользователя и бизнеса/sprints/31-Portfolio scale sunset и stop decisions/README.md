# Спринт 31. Portfolio, scale, sunset и stop decisions

Ориентировочный объём: 19–28 часов.

## Центральный вопрос

Когда следует масштабировать, изменить, объединить, остановить или закрыть продукт/инициативу?

## Результат спринта

После завершения необходимо уметь:

- distinguish initiative, product, capability and portfolio;
- evaluate evidence maturity and strategic fit;
- compare scale, maintain, pivot, merge, sunset and stop options;
- account for sunk-cost bias and political ownership;
- define scale readiness across demand, economics, operations and risk;
- identify cannibalization and portfolio overlap;
- plan sunset, migration, data retention and customer communication;
- measure opportunity cost and capacity release;
- create kill criteria before escalation;
- preserve learning after closure.

## Основной маршрут

1. Compare four synthetic product bets.
2. Assess strategic fit, evidence, economics and risk.
3. Make scale/maintain/pivot/stop decisions.
4. Design one respectful sunset and migration.

## Лабораторная работа

Создать `work/portfolio-decisions/`.

### Requirements

- each initiative has strategic role and evidence maturity;
- scale decision requires demand, unit economics, support and continuity readiness;
- sunk cost is excluded from future-value reasoning;
- overlapping products/capabilities are identified;
- stop/sunset criteria are explicit and predeclared where possible;
- migration covers data, contract, support and accessibility needs;
- customer communication and alternatives are defined;
- released capacity and follow-up ownership are visible;
- lessons are preserved without keeping zombie systems.

### Failure scenarios

1. Investment continues because much has already been spent.
2. Strong engagement but negative unit economics is scaled.
3. Product is shut down without migration/export.
4. Strategic pet project has no kill criteria.
5. Portfolio duplicates the same capability across teams.
6. Sunset leaves support/security/data obligations ownerless.

## Результат для базы знаний

- **Product portfolio and initiative decisions**.
- **Scale readiness and evidence maturity**.
- **Pivot, maintain, merge, sunset and stop options**.
- **Sunk cost, opportunity cost and kill criteria**.
- **Product sunset and customer migration**.

## Когда переходить дальше

Можно переходить, когда continue/scale/stop decisions are based on future value, evidence, economics and obligations rather than sunk cost or ownership politics.
