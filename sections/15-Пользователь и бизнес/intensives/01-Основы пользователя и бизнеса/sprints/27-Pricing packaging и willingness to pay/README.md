# Спринт 27. Pricing, packaging и willingness to pay

Ориентировочный объём: 18–27 часов.

## Центральный вопрос

Как связать price and package with customer value, usage, costs and buying constraints without confusing stated preference with actual willingness to pay?

## Результат спринта

После завершения необходимо уметь:

- distinguish price, packaging, metric and discount policy;
- identify value metric and buyer budget context;
- compare per-seat, usage, tier, subscription and service models;
- analyze price fairness, predictability and gaming;
- design packaging by segment and capability;
- estimate willingness-to-pay evidence cautiously;
- understand limitations of surveys and pricing interviews;
- model discount, contract and sales complexity;
- evaluate pricing impact on adoption, margin and support;
- avoid dark patterns and surprise charges.

## Основной маршрут

1. Identify customer value and cost drivers.
2. Compare four pricing metrics/models.
3. Create three packages with target segments.
4. Simulate WTP research and revenue/margin outcomes.
5. Define pilot and change safeguards.

## Лабораторная работа

Создать `work/pricing-packaging/`.

### Requirements

- pricing metric correlates with value and is measurable/auditable;
- packages serve distinct segments/jobs rather than arbitrary feature fences;
- cost and margin consequences are modeled;
- buyer predictability and procurement constraints are included;
- WTP evidence source and limitations are explicit;
- discounts have rationale, authority and expiry;
- price changes include communication, grandfathering/migration and cancellation rights;
- accessibility/essential functions are not exploitatively gated;
- sensitivity analysis covers adoption and usage shifts.

### Failure scenarios

1. Price copies competitor without value/cost context.
2. Per-seat model discourages intended collaboration.
3. Usage metric creates unpredictable bills.
4. Survey answer is treated as actual purchase behavior.
5. Discounts permanently destroy price integrity.
6. Package complexity increases support/sales cost beyond gain.

## Результат для базы знаний

- **Pricing metrics and business models**.
- **Product packaging by segment and value**.
- **Willingness-to-pay research limitations**.
- **Pricing migration, fairness and predictability**.

## Когда переходить дальше

Можно переходить, когда price/package choices are coherent with value, cost, buying behavior and migration risk across target segments.
