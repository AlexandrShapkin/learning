# Спринт 03. Интервью, observation и contextual inquiry

Ориентировочный объём: 18–27 часов.

## Центральный вопрос

Как понять реальную работу и поведение пользователей, не подменяя их воспоминаниями, мнениями или желаемым поведением?

## Результат спринта

После завершения необходимо уметь:

- готовить interview guide around past behavior and context;
- задавать neutral probes and follow-up questions;
- различать claimed and observed behavior;
- проводить contextual inquiry and task observation;
- фиксировать workflow, tools, workarounds and constraints;
- отделять notes, quotes, interpretation and insight;
- синтезировать patterns without overgeneralization;
- защищать privacy and participant comfort;
- распознавать interviewer bias and social desirability.

## Основной маршрут

1. Create a guide for dispatcher and field-worker sessions.
2. Run synthetic interview transcripts and one observed workflow.
3. Code observations by behavior, context, pain and workaround.
4. Compare claimed versus observed behavior.

## Лабораторная работа

Создать `work/interviews-observation/`.

### Requirements

- questions focus on recent concrete examples;
- leading, hypothetical and solution-pitch questions are minimized;
- observation records sequence, tools, interruptions and exceptions;
- notes separate verbatim evidence from inference;
- at least two contradictory cases are preserved;
- sample limitations are explicit;
- one initial belief is revised or rejected;
- participant data is pseudonymized and bounded.

### Failure scenarios

1. “Would you use this feature?” is treated as demand evidence.
2. Interviewer explains or sells the idea during discovery.
3. Only polished success stories are captured.
4. Observation ignores workarounds and interruptions.
5. One quote becomes a universal insight.
6. Contradictory evidence is discarded.

## Результат для базы знаний

- **Behavioral user interviews**.
- **Contextual inquiry and workflow observation**.
- **Claimed versus observed behavior**.
- **Research-note coding and synthesis**.
- **Interviewer and social-desirability bias**.

## Когда переходить дальше

Можно переходить, когда insights are grounded in concrete past behavior and observed context, including contradictions and sample limits.
