# Спринт 12. Prototype, MVP, pilot и validation

Ориентировочный объём: 19–28 часов.

## Центральный вопрос

Какой минимальный intervention нужен, чтобы проверить ключевую uncertainty without prematurely building or scaling the full product?

## Результат спринта

После завершения необходимо уметь:

- distinguish prototype, concierge test, fake door, MVP and pilot;
- choose validation method by uncertainty type;
- define learning goal, participant scope and success/stop criteria;
- separate desirability, usability, feasibility and viability evidence;
- design ethical tests without deception or harm;
- bound operational/manual work;
- avoid MVP as low-quality full product;
- plan transition from pilot to scale or shutdown;
- capture evidence and limitations.

## Основной маршрут

1. Identify the riskiest assumption in one product bet.
2. Compare prototype, concierge and pilot options.
3. Build one synthetic validation test.
4. Evaluate result and choose continue, adapt or stop.

## Лабораторная работа

Создать `work/prototype-pilot/`.

### Requirements

- validation target and uncertainty are explicit;
- method produces the needed evidence with minimal build;
- participants and exposure are bounded;
- success, guardrail and stop criteria are predeclared;
- manual/concierge effort is measured and not hidden;
- test does not promise unavailable capability or manipulate users;
- result separates usability from demand/viability;
- scale assumptions and shutdown/cleanup are documented.

### Failure scenarios

1. MVP means the full roadmap with poor quality.
2. Prototype usability is interpreted as market demand.
3. Fake-door test misleads users without safeguards.
4. Concierge operation cannot scale but cost is ignored.
5. Pilot includes only enthusiasts and excludes hard cases.
6. Success criteria are changed after results.

## Результат для базы знаний

- **Prototypes, MVPs and pilots**.
- **Desirability, usability, feasibility and viability evidence**.
- **Concierge and fake-door validation ethics**.
- **Pilot-to-scale and shutdown decisions**.

## Когда переходить дальше

Можно переходить, когда the smallest ethical test resolves a named uncertainty and produces a clear continue/adapt/stop decision.
